Research Papers

RoASt is built on a foundation of peer-reviewed research in distributed robotics and algorithm synthesis. This page lists related publications and provides context for the theoretical foundations of the tool.

Publications

Synthesizing Robot Algorithms Through Progressive Validation

Author A, Author B, Author C • International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA) • 2024

We present a methodology for synthesizing robot algorithms using progressive validation by levels, enabling systematic exploration of algorithm spaces while maintaining correctness guarantees.

Classification of Distributed Robot Algorithms by Execution Metrics

Author A, Author D • Journal of Autonomous Robots • 2023

This paper introduces a comprehensive classification system for distributed robot algorithms based on 23 distinct metric families, enabling systematic comparison and analysis.

Local Views and Global Behaviors in Multi-Robot Systems

Author B, Author C, Author E • Distributed Computing • 2022

We analyze the relationship between local robot perceptions and emergent global behaviors, establishing theoretical foundations for algorithm synthesis tools.
